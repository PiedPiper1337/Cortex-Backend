Sending from App to Backend:
    Carrier:Type:HexEncodedPrimaryKey:ActualMessageOrQuestion

    example -
    ATT:QUES:1b:who is the tenth president


Sending from Backend to App:
    CHexEncodedPrimaryKey:currentMessage#/TotalMessage#:Answer

    example -
    C1b:1/1:John Tyler


Note that the hexencodedprimarykey will always be prepended with 'C'
